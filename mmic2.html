Eid-ul-Fitr Mubarak

Acts to be performed on Eid-ul-Fitr:

  • Making du'a on the night of Eid
  • Paying Sadaqatul Fitr before Eid Salah
  • Having Ghusl before Eid Salah
  • Eating Dates before going to the Eid Salah
  • Reciting Takbeer on the way to the Eid Salah (الله اكبر الله اكبر لا اله الا الله والله اكبر الله اكبر ولله الحمد)
  • Congratulating each other
